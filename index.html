About

I am currently a graduate student majoring in computer vision at Carnegie Mellon University. I obtained the Bachelor's degrees in computer science from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (First Class Honors), where I worked with Prof. Chi Keung Tang, Prof. Qifeng Chen and Prof. Yu-Wing Tai. My interests include computer vision, machine learning and data science. The area I primarily focus on is computer vision application which applies vision and graphics to novel products. Previously, I was a research intern on image enhancement at SenseTime and on network pruning at Tencent YouTu Lab.
